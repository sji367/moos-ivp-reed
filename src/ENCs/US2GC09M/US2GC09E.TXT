Numerous oil well structures exist in the water areas from Key West, Florida, to Brazos Santiago, Texas. Some wells are submerged and capped. Only those reported submerged structures of unknown depth and submerged structures covered less than 20.1 meter/11 fathoms are charted outside of the 18.2 meter/10 fathom curve. See charts in the 1:400,000 scale range and 1:80000 scale series charts for location of surface wells and wells submerged 20.1 meter/11 fathoms or more, aids to navigation and safety fairways thru oil fields.
