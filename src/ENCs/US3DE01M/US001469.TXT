The controlling depth within the Five Fathom Bank to Cape Henlopen in-
bound traffic lane is 12.1 meters/40 feet. The Marine Advisory Committee recommends 
that vessels with drafts of 10.7 meters/35 feet or greater use the Delaware to Cape 
Henlopen inbound traffic lane.
The controlling depth within the Cape Henlopen to Five Fathom Bank 
outbound traffic lane is 13.1meters/43 feet.
