SAN FRANCISCO-OAKLAND BAY BRIDGE
(Privately maintained aids)
The piers are lettered on the chart for reference.
Lights:
Spans BC,CD and IJ. Fixed green lights on each side of the bridge
over the center of the channel.

CAUTION:
Mid-span clearance under the long 
spans of the San Francisco-Oakland Bay Bridge 
are approximate and at a temperature of 55°F.  
These clearances may be reduced several feet 
due to extreme traffic conditions and a prolonged 
period of abnormally high temperature.
