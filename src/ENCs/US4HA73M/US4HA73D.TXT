PARTICULARLY SENSITIVE SEA AREA (PSSA): This chart falls entirely within the limits of a Particularly
Sensitive Sea Area (PSSA). A PSSA is an environmentally sensitive area in which and around which
mariners should exercise extreme caution. See U.S. Coast Pilot volumes for information regarding
this area.
