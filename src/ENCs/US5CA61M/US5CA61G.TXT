The Los Angeles and Long Beach main channels are considered 
narrow channels. Vessels less than 20 meters in length, sailing vessels, 
vessels engaged in fishing, or any vessel attempting to cross these 
channels shall not impede a vessel that can only safely navigate within 
a narrow channel as per Inland Navigation Rules, Rule 9. To obtain 
information on the movement of deep draft vessels inside the Federal 
Breakwater, contact the Los Angeles Pilot Station on channel 73
(156.675MHz) / ph 310-732-3805 or Long Beach Pilot Station on channel 
74(156.6MHz) / ph 562-432-0664.
