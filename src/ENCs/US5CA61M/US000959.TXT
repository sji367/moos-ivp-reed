A precautionary area has been established in the Los Angeles - Long Beach area. 
Large vessels are maneuvering to embark or disembark pilots; and to enter or depart 
the traffic separation system. It is recommended vessels proceed with extreme caution 
in this area. 
Vessels are prohibited from anchoring in the precautionary area except in desig-
nated anchorages (110.214 - see note A).
