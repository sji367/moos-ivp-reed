NOTE B
The U.S Coast Guard operates a mandatory Vessel Traffic 
Services (VTS) system in the U.S waters covered by this 
chart. Vessel operating procedures and designated radio-
telephone frequencies are published in 33 CFR 161, the 
U.S. Coast Pilot, and/or in the VTS User's Manual.

NOTE C
A Cooperative Vessel Traffic Services (CVTS) system 
has been established by the United States and Canada 
within the adjoining waters in the Juan de Fuca Region.
The appropriate Vessel Traffic Center (VTC) (Tofino Traffic, 
Seattle Traffic, and Vincouver Traffic) administers the rules 
issued by both nations, however, it will enforce only its 
own set of rules within its jurisdiction.
