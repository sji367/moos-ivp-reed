CAUTION - QUALITY OF BATHYMETRIC DATA

The area represented by the object M_QUAL are approximate due to the generalizing for clarity.  
Caution is advised, particularly for nearshore navigation or voyage planning.  
M_QUAL (Quality of data) represents area of uniform quality of bathymetric data.  
The CATZOC (Category of zone of confidence in data) attribute of M_QUAL provides an assessment of the overall zone of confidence.
