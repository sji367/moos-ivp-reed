The Gulf Gateway Deepwater Port at 28°05'16.20"N,
093°03'07.20"W is surrounded by three concentric
circles. The first is a Safety Zone that has a 500
meter radius. The second is a mandatory No
Anchoring Area that has a 1500 meter radius and
the third is an Area to be Avoided that has a 2000
meter radius. The Safety Zone has been
promulgated, via an Interim Rule published on
May 11, 2005. Reference IMO SN/Circ.240.
