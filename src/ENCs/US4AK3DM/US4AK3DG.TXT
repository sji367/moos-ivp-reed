         NON-MOTORIZED WATERS

Navigation by motorized vessels is prohibited in the followings areas:
Upper Muir Inlet, June 1 - July 15
Wachusett Inlet, July 16 - Aug. 31
Adams Inlet, May 1 - Sep. 15
Rendu Inlet, May 1 - Sep. 15
Hugh Miller Inlet (including Scidmore Bay and Charpentier Inlet), May 1 - Sep. 15
Beardslee Islands complex, May 1 - Sep. 15
A full description of the regulation may be found in the U.S. Coast Pilot.
