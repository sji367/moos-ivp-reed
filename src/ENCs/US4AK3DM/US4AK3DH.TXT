                      JOHNS HOPKINS INLET

Johns Hopkins Inlet is closed to all vessels, including kayaks, May 1 -
June 30, for protection of newborn harbor seal pups. The inlet is closed to
cruise ships May 1 - Sep. 1. Other vessels may enter the area after July 1 at
a speed of no more than 10 knots.
A full description of the regulation may be found in the U.S. Coast Pilot.
