NOTE D

All Mariners are requested to exercise caution when navigating Hinchinbrook 
Entrance, Prince William Sound. There are heavy concentrations of crab pots and
related gear, and numerous fishing vessels that are operating in the area of
Hinchinbrook Entrance, Seal Rocks and between Seal Rocks and Montague
Island. Vessels entering or leaving Prince William Sound through Hinchinbrook
Entrance are requested to utilize the recommended track.
