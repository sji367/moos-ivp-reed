CAUTION - VESSELS
Vessels entering or departing Kodiak/St. Paul
Harbor with an air draft of 138 feet/42 meters or higher
require operating changes for aircraft at the
Kodiak airport and should notify the Federal
Aviation Administration's (FAA) Anchorage Air
Route Traffic Control Center at (907) 269-1103 of
their presence. Additional information is available
in Coast Pilot 9.
