Traffic within the Precautionary Area may consists of vessels making the transition between the Main Ship
Channel and one of the established traffic lanes. Mariners are advised to exercise extreme caution when
navigating within this area. The normal cruising area of the pilot vessel is indicated "PILOT AREA". When
passing Traffic Lane Lighted Buoys "S", "W", or "N", inbound vessels should contact the pilot boat on
channel 13 for boarding instructions.
