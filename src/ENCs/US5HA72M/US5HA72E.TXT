PAPAHANAUMOKUAKEA MARINE NATIONAL MONUMENT:
The entire area of this chart lies within the boundaries of the
Papahanaumokuakea Marine National Monument and the Gardinar Pinnacles
Special Preservation Area.  These are protected area. See 50 CFR 404
or Chapter 2, U.S. Coast Pilot 7. 

PARTICULARLY SENSITIVE SEA AREA:
This chart falls entirely within the limts of a Particularly Sensitive Sea Area
(PSSA). A PSSA is an environmentally sensitive area in which and around which
mariners should exercise extreme caution. See U.S. Coast Pilot volumes for 
information regarding this area.
