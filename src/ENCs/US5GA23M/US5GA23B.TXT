Soundings are in feet. Those south of New Savannah Bluff
Dam refer to the low water plane which corresponds to a
discharge at the dam of 172.7 cubic meter/6,100 cubic feet per second.
Soundings north of new Savannah Bluff Dam refer to the
normal pool level elevation which is 34.9meter/114.5 feet above Mean
Sea Level.
The Corps of Engineers project depth is 2.7 meters/9 feet at the
established low water planes.
