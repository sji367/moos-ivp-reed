Project Depths
3.6 meters/12 feet Norfolk, VA to Fort Pierce FL; 3.0 meters/10 feet Fort Pierce, FL to Miami FL; 2.1 meters/7 feet Miami, FL to Cross Bank, Florida Bay. The controlling depths are published periodically in the U.S. Coast Guard Local Notice to Mariners.

DISTANCES
The Waterway is indicated by DISMARs (distance marks). Mileage distances shown along the Waterway are in Statute Miles, southward from Norfolk, VA. Tables for converting Statute Miles to International Nautical Miles are given in U.S. Coast Pilot 4.
