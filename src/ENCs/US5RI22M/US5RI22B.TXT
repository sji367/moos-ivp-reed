A Naval Restricted area lies within the East passage 
between Conanicut Island and Prudence Island with a southern limit .3 nautical miles south of Gould 
Island and a northern limit approximately 1 nautical mile north of Hope Island. No person or vessel 
shall at any time, under any circumstances, anchor or fish or tow a drag of any kind in the prohibited 
area because of the extensive cable system located therein.  See Chapter 2 of this Coast Pilot for 
additional information.
