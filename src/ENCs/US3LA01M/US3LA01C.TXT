The controlling depth was 2.4 metres/8 feet for a width of 24.4 metres/80 feet from Loreauville Canal to Dauterive Lake. The channel is marked by red beacons "2" through "90" with intermediate aids not charted. 
A continuous spoil area, with marked 15.2 metres/50 foot openings every 609.6 metres/2,000 feet, exists to the east and south of the channel.
