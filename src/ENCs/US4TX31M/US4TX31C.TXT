The channel for the Intracoastal Waterway through Matagorda Bay is controlled to 10 feet, due to the presence of several pipelines crossing the channel at approximate positions 28°27’31.21”N, 96°22’59.2”W; 28°27’30.3”N, 96°22’59.6”W; and 28°27’11.5”N, 96°23’10.0”W. Mariners should be aware of the draft limitations in this channel.
