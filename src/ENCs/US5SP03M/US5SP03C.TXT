Currents in Harbor Entrance
Maximum flood current of 1.5 knots, setting North to Northeast.
Maximum ebb current of 3 knots setting Southwest.
Slack water ocurrs 30 min. before low and 45 min. before high water.
The prevalent set of the current in harbor entrance is generally to the South or Southwest regardless of the tide, but a set to the North or Northeast may be experienced, especially during summer months.
