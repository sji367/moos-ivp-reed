Traffic within the Precautionary Area may consist of 
vessels making the transition between the Main Ship 
Channel and one of the established traffic lanes.  
Mariners are advised to exercise extreme caution when 
navigating within this area. The normal cruising area 
of the pilot vessel is indicated "PILOT AREA." When 
passing Traffic Lane Lighted buoys "S", "W", or "N", 
inbound vessels should contact the pilot boat on 
Channel 13 for boarding instructions.
