Project depths for the Intracoastal Waterway (ICW) are 3.6 meters/12 feet from Norfolk, 
Virginia to Fort Pierce, Florida 10 feet from Fort Pierce, Florida to Miami, 
Florida and 2.1meters/7 feet from Miami, Florida to Cross Bank, Florida Bay.  The 
controlling depths are published periodically in the U.S. Coast Guard Local 
Notice to Mariners.  Mileage distances along the ICW are in Statute Miles, 
southward from Norfolk, Virginia.  Tables for converting Statute Miles to 
International Nautical Miles are given in U.S. Coast Pilot 4.
