Entry into this zone is prohibited unless authorized by the Coast Guard Captain of the Port of Toledo. Persons desiring to transit through this security zone, prior to transiting, must contact the Captain of the Port of Toledo at telephone number (419)-418-6050, or on VHF/FM channel 16 and request permission. If permission is granted, all persons and vessels shall comply with the instructions of the Captain of the Port or his or her designated representative.
