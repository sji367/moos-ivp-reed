The Inland Navigational Rules Act of 1980 is in effect for
vessels transiting this area. The seaward boundaries of this
area are the COLREGS demarcation lines. In the area seaward
of the COLREGS demarcation lines, vessels are governed by COLREGS:
International Regulations for Prevention Collisions at Sea, 1972.
The COLREGS demarcation lines are defined in 33 CFR 80.720a,
33 CFR 80.720b, 33 CFR 80.720c, 33 CFR 80.717i, 33 CFR 80.717h,
and 33 CFR 80.717g and 33 CFR 80.717j.
