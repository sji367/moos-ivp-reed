Deep draft vessels entering and departing Penobscot Bay and River are 
requested to remain within the Recommended Vessel Route. Two-way traffic is 
possible within all parts of the Recommended Vessel Route. Other vessels, while not 
excluded, should exercise caution in these areas and monitor VHF channel 16 
or 13 for information concerning vessels transiting these areas. See U.S. Coast 
Pilot 1, Chapter 7.
