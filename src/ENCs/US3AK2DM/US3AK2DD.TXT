Significant changes in depth and shoreline have 
occurred in the area of this chart as a result of the 
earthquake of March 27, 1964. Tidal observations 
since the earthquake indicate bottom subsidence of 
-1.1 meters/-3.5 feet at Seward. Mariners are urged to use 
extreme caution when navigating in the area of this 
chart as the magnitude of the change except at this 
site is not known.
