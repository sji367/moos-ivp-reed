The Project depth for South Pass is 5.1 meters/17 feet and for the
Southwest pass is 13.7 meters/45 feet through Head of Passes. Mariners
are advised that there is severe shoaling at South Pass.
Shoaling to bare at MLLW was found in June of 2014. The 
controlling depths and regulations may be obtained from
the Office of the District Engineer, Crops of Engineers in
New Orleans.
