Hydrography including soundings, rocks, and shoals in the area identified in the Source Diagram as Miscellaneous was transferred from smaller scale chart 16220. Hydrography from 16220 was taken from numerous ship tracks or older charts, including Russian charts. Sufficient data is not available to correct depths to a common datum. Investigations reveal that 12 to 18 feet should be added to many depths to adjust them to Mean Lower Low Water.
