Project depths for the Intracoastal Waterway (ICW) are 3.6 meters/12 feet from Norfolk, 
Virginia to Fort Pierce, Florida 10 feet from Fort Pierce, Florida to Miami, 
Florida and 2.1 meters/7 feet from Miami, Florida to Cross Bank, Florida Bay.  Consult 
the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers for controlling depths and U.S. Coast Guard Local Notice 
to Mariners for other navigation hazards or restrctions.  Mileage distances along the ICW 
are in Statute Miles, southward from Norfolk, Virginia.  Tables for converting Statute Miles to 
International Nautical Miles are given in U.S. Coast Pilot 4.
