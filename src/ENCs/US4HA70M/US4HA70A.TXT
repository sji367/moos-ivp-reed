PARTICULARLY SENSITIVE SEA AREA
A PSSA is an environmentally sensitive area in which and around which mariners should exercise extreme caution. 
See U.S. Coast Pilot volume 7 for information regarding this area.

PAPAHANAUMOKUAKEA MARINE NATIONAL MONUMENT - FRENCH FRIGATE SHOALS SPECIAL PRESERVATION AREA
The Papahanaumokuakea Marine National Monument and the French Frigate Shoals Special Preservation Area, are protected areas.
See 50 CFR 404 or Chapter 2, U.S. Coast Pilot 7.
