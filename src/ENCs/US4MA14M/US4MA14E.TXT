Yellow low pressure sodium lights are
installed on the north and south banks, except for
the south bank between the highway bridges,
which has white mercury vapor lamps. The lights
are located 42.6 meters/140 feet from the edge of the channel
and in general are 152.4 meters/500 feet apart on both banks.

Traffic lights are maintained at the Cape Cod Bay entrance
to the canal for westbound vessels, and at Wings Neck for
eastbound vessels.
Information on operating conditions is available by
telephone or radio at the Cape Cod Canal office, Buzzards Bay.
For detailed information consult monthly bulletins published
by the Corps of Engineers, Concord, MA.
